FaceGen Viewport

The viewport is the 3D view on the left

  • Mouse
    • Rotate: left-click-drag
    • Scale: right-click-drag
    • Move: middle-click-drag OR shift-left-click-drag
  • Touchscreen
    • Rotate: touch-drag
    • Scale: pinch
  • Drag and drop FaceGen .FG files here to update the face
  • For more viewport controls go to the View tab
